## About the Project

CSR Activities is a dedicated digital platform designed to showcase and promote impactful social development initiatives undertaken through collaborative efforts between educational institutions, communities, and government-supported programs.

Developed under the guidance of the Higher & Technical Education Department, Government of Maharashtra, in collaboration with the Maharashtra Information Technology Support Centre (MITSC), the platform highlights projects focused on community welfare, youth empowerment, skill development, entrepreneurship, and sustainable social transformation.

---

## Vision

To create a socially responsible and self-reliant society by connecting education, innovation, and community-driven development initiatives.

---

## Key Objectives

- Promote community development initiatives
- Encourage youth entrepreneurship and self-employment
- Support skill development programs
- Showcase social impact projects
- Foster sustainable and inclusive growth
- Strengthen educational and societal collaboration
