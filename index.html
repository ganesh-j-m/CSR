Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

Driving youth empowerment, entrepreneurship, and rural livelihood upgrades across Maharashtra by blending practical education with structural social welfare.

Experiential Entrepreneurship

College Campus Food Vans Project

Under the umbrella of Career Katta, this unique initiative introduces specialized Food Vans within college premises. It transitions students from theoretical business models to real-world commercial operations.

Earn While You Learn Framework

Students actively manage food production, inventory control, and retail processes, gaining real confidence to become self-reliant entrepreneurs.

Pilot Phase & Scaled Expansion

Successfully launched on an experimental scale across Nagpur, Pune, and Mumbai, with a long-term goal of operationalizing at least 5 food vans in every district.

Rural Agro-Development

'Madhur Kranti' Sweet Revolution Campaign

A collaborative mission launched by the Khadi and Village Industries Board (KVIB) and MITSC. It connects modern student communities with agricultural ecosystems through scientific apiculture.

Scientific Training & Infrastructure

Moving past simple theoretical knowledge to provide complete practical setups, installing 25 bee-boxes per college across 100 selected institutes in the state.

Boosting Rural Livelihoods

Empowers over 35,000 to 40,000 students annually with scientific skills, creating eco-friendly self-employment while helping local farmers increase crop yields and income.

Ecosystem Pillars

Strategic Program Expectations

How public-private alignment ensures structural, financial, and environmental sustainability across regional sectors.

Academic & Industry Blending

Aligning academic learning with concrete commercial systems, helping youth build active business minds rather than relying solely on traditional theoretical tracks.

Institutional Leadership

The active support and coordination of college Principals and Coordinators are vital to effectively deploy and run these intensive field training blueprints on ground.

Socio-Economic Upgrades

Utilizing advanced logistical assistance through MITSC to implement tech-driven monitoring tools, helping rural families build eco-friendly and long-term financial stability.
